Namaskaara to everybody

Bangalore is a great confluence of cultures. People from all over the country come to settle here. We all know about its cosmopolitan nature. But once you are here, you realize that understanding Kannada is one of those things that, while not necessary in our day to day life, would make so many things so much simpler.

Sometimes it's just talking to your maid in a language she understands, and sometimes its to get you a fare a hundred rupees less on a friday night. Sometimes you just get along better with your Kannadiga friends speaking in the native tongue.

Sometimes it's just about the love for learning languages. Language for language sake. And Kannada is a beautiful language that opens out many forms of culture and art to your understanding.

Whatever be your reasons for learning the language, you won't be disappointed with Kannadify. We will take you through a form of study called as immersive learning. This is how languages are learnt best. Through reading, speaking and hearing. And having someone do that with you, albeit electronically, helps hugely in the process

So what are you waiting for? Jump Right in!
