Namaskaara to everybody,

Thank you all for signing up to learn Kannada.

This time, I will not be doing much writing. Instead, I will share a short audio clip (~2 minutes long) with you as frequently as possible. I hope all of you will take the time to listen to it the very day it is sent.

idea behind these audios is that constantly listening to Kannada will help you pick up its accent, its pronunciation, its common words and phrases, etc. With every audio, I will include the English transcript.

So please do not worry if you don’t fully understand what I’m saying. I am confident that you will soon begin to find certain “sounds” and words familiar!

(However, if you think it is becoming too much, do let me know immediately!)
